Royal Score - Rules & How to Play

Game Objective

Draw cards, score points based on your highest card, and try to beat the high score! The game ends when the deck runs out.

How to Play

  • Click Draw to draw 5 cards from the deck.
  • Review your cards displayed on the table.
  • Click Score to score your hand. Your score is determined by the highest card drawn.
  • Repeat: Draw, then Score, until the deck is empty.
  • Try to beat your previous high score!

Rules

  • You must score your hand before drawing again.
  • You can only score if cards are visible on the table.
  • The highest card in your hand determines your score for that round.
  • When the deck runs out, the game ends and your final score is compared to the high score.

Scoring

Each card rank has a point value. Higher cards score more points. For example:
2 = 10 pts, 10 = 100 pts, Jack = 125 pts, Queen = 150 pts, King = 200 pts, Ace = 400 pts.
