## ğŸ² How to Play

### Solo Mode
1. Draw 5 cards
2. Score based on your highest card
3. Beat your high score!

### Multiplayer
1. Create or join a room
2. Wait for players to ready up
3. Take turns drawing and scoring
4. Highest score after 10 rounds wins!
