üé¥ Royal Score - Rules & How to Play

üéØ Game Objective

Royal Score is a card game where you draw cards, score points based on your hand, and compete for the highest score! The game features both Solo and Multiplayer modes. Test your luck and strategy to achieve the best possible score.

üéÆ Solo Mode

  • Play against yourself
  • Beat your high score
  • Draw until deck is empty
  • Perfect for practice!

üë• Multiplayer Mode

  • Play with 2-6 friends
  • 10 rounds per game
  • Take turns drawing
  • Highest score wins!

üìñ How to Play

Solo Mode

  1. Click Draw to draw 5 cards from a double deck (104 cards total).
  2. Review your cards displayed on the table.
  3. Click Score to score your hand. Your score is based on the highest card in your hand.
  4. Repeat the Draw ‚Üí Score cycle until the deck runs out (about 20 rounds).
  5. Try to beat your previous high score!

Multiplayer Mode

  1. Create a Room: Enter your name, select max players, and create a room to get a 6-character code.
  2. Join a Room: Enter your name and the room code shared by your friend.
  3. Ready Up: All players must click "Ready Up" to start the game.
  4. Take Turns: Players take turns drawing 5 cards and scoring their hands.
  5. 10 Rounds: The game lasts for 10 rounds. The player with the highest total score wins!

üìã Game Rules

Rule 1: You must score your current hand before drawing new cards.
Rule 2: You can only score if cards are visible on the table.
Rule 3: The highest card in your hand determines your score for that round (suits don't matter).
Rule 4: In Solo mode, the game ends when the deck runs out. In Multiplayer, the game ends after 10 rounds.
Rule 5: In Multiplayer mode, you can only draw and score on your turn. Wait for others to complete their turns.

üí° Tips & Strategy

  • Play the Odds: In Solo mode with 104 cards (2 decks), there are 8 Aces total. You have better odds than you think!
  • Solo Strategy: With ~20 rounds available in Solo mode, aim for an average of 150+ points per round to reach 3000+ total.
  • Consistency Wins: In Multiplayer, steady scoring can beat occasional high draws over 10 rounds.
  • Track the Deck: Pay attention to cards already played to estimate remaining high cards.
  • Face Card Focus: Kings (200 pts) and Aces (400 pts) make the biggest impact. Each face card you draw adds significant points.
  • Multiplayer Tactics: Watch your opponents' scores to know when you need to take risks. With only 10 rounds, every turn counts!
